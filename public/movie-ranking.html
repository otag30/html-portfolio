My top 3 movies of all-time.


Avatar

Avatar (2009) - A visually stunning science fiction epic directed by James Cameron that explores themes of environmentalism and colonialism.

Set in the mid-22nd century, the film follows Jake Sully, a paraplegic former Marine who is selected to participate in the Avatar Program, which allows humans to remotely control genetically engineered Na'vi bodies on the alien moon of Pandora.
As Jake immerses himself in the Na'vi culture and forms a bond with Neytiri, he becomes torn between his loyalty to the human military forces seeking to exploit Pandora's resources and his growing connection to the Na'vi people and their environment.
As tensions escalate, Jake must choose between two worlds in a battle for survival and freedom.

Fracture

Fracture (2007) - A gripping legal thriller that explores themes of justice and morality.

The film centers on Ted Crawford, a wealthy engineer who shoots his unfaithful wife and then confidently confesses to the crime.
However, when ambitious young prosecutor Willy Beachum takes on the case, he discovers that Crawford has carefully crafted a complex defense to exploit legal loopholes.
As Willy digs deeper, he finds himself entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where Crawford's intellect challenges Willy’s legal prowess.
The film culminates in a tense courtroom showdown that tests both men’s resolve and moral boundaries.

Silence of the Lambs

(1991) - A psychological thriller that introduced the iconic characters of Clarice Starling and Hannibal Lecter.

Silence of the Lambs follows FBI trainee Clarice Starling, who is tasked with interviewing the imprisoned Dr. Hannibal Lecter, a brilliant psychiatrist and cannibalistic serial killer.
The FBI hopes Lecter can provide insight into the mind of another serial killer, known as "Buffalo Bill," who is abducting and murdering young women.

As Clarice engages in a psychological cat-and-mouse game with Lecter, she confronts her own traumatic past. Lecter, intrigued by Clarice, offers cryptic clues about Buffalo Bill’s identity in exchange for personal details about her life.
As the tension builds, Clarice must race against time to save the latest victim before it’s too late, ultimately leading to a chilling climax that tests her courage and resolve.
